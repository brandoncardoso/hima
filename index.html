This is Hima, a simple Jekyll theme with the goal of being accessible to everyone. It follows The A11Y Project’s accessibility guidelines. It comes with a light and dark colour scheme, as well as an adaptive colour scheme that switches between light and dark depending on your OS/browser settings.

For how to install and other information, check out the Hima repository on GitHub.
